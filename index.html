  • Chicken Breasts
  • Zesty spice blend
  • Barbecue sauce
  • Avocado slices
  • Grilled pineapple slices

    • Steps to Make The Ultimate Programmer's Grilled Chicken

    1. Season chicken breasts with zesty spice blend.
    2. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
    3. Grill chicken until cooked through, about 6-8 minutes per side.
    4. Brush chicken with barbecue sauce during the last few minutes of grilling.
    5. Serve grilled chicken topped with avocado slices and grilled pineapple slices. Enjoy!
