Ingredients

  • Artisanal dough
  • Tangy marinara sauce
  • Gooey mozzarella cheese
  • Crispy Turkey Bacon
  • Aromatic truffle oil

The Simple 5 Steps To Make That Pizza

  • Preheat your oven to the highest temperature possible, typically around 500°F (260°C), and place a pizza stone or baking sheet inside to heat up.
  • Roll out the artisanal dough on a lightly floured surface into your desired shape, ensuring it's thin and even.
  • Spread a generous layer of tangy marinara sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  • Sprinkle the gooey mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce, then add the crispy bacon on top.
  • Drizzle the entire pizza with aromatic truffle oil, then carefully transfer it onto the preheated pizza stone or baking sheet. Bake in the oven for 8-10 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbling and melted. Remove from the oven, slice, and enjoy the symphony of flavors!
